Her Story

A short tribute that captures who she was.

Jane Elizabeth Carter lived with grace, deep compassion, and a gift for making people feel at home. She was the steady center of her family, the first to offer encouragement, and the last to ask for anything in return.

She loved morning light, handwritten notes, garden roses, family dinners, and long talks that turned into laughter. Whether through quiet acts of service or unforgettable celebrations, Jane had a way of making ordinary days feel meaningful.

Her legacy lives on in every tradition she started, every lesson she passed down, and every person who was better for knowing her.

Favorite Things

Known for: Warm hugs, generous hospitality, thoughtful advice

Loved: Family gatherings, gardening, music, Sunday dinners

Legacy: A home filled with love and a life that brought people together
